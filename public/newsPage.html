New! Competition!

Take part in the competition from our store Pages&Co.

Rules

  1. Eligibility:
    The contest is open to children aged 5 to 18 years.
    Participants may submit entries individually or as a team (maximum of 2 children per team).
  2. Contest Theme:
    Create an original children’s story, accompanied by illustrations. The theme can be anything that sparks imagination and creativity.
  3. Story Requirements:
    The story must be between 300 and 1000 words. It should be suitable for children and convey a positive message or lesson.
  4. Illustration Requirements:
    Participants must create at least 3 illustrations that relate to the story.
    Illustrations can be in any medium (drawings, paintings, digital art, etc.) but should be scanned or photographed for submission.
  5. Submission Guidelines:
    Both the story and illustrations should be submitted via email to [email@example.com].
    The subject line of the email must read: Pages&Co. Contest Submission - [Participant's Name(s)]".
    All entries must be submitted by [Insert Deadline Date].
  6. Judging Criteria:
    Stories will be judged based on creativity, originality, writing quality, and the ability to engage young readers. Illustrations will be judged on creativity, relevance to the story, and visual appeal.
    A panel of judges from HarperCollins and Pages&Co. will make the final decision.
  7. Prize:
    The winner(s) will receive a £5000 gift certificate to use at Pages&Co. store.
    The winning story may also be featured on the Pages&Co. website and social media platforms (with parental permission).
  8. Notification:
    Winners will be announced on [Insert Announcement Date] via email and on our social media channels.
    The winning participant(s) must respond to the notification within 7 days to claim their prize.
  9. Rights:
    By submitting an entry, participants grant Pages&Co. and HarperCollins the right to share the story and illustrations for promotional purposes.
  10. Contact Information:
    For any inquiries regarding the contest, please contact us at [pages_and_co@gmail.com].

    11. We look forward to your delightful stories and amazing illustrations! Happy writing!

    Sponsored by HarperCollins.
